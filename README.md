# 🛒 Superstore Sales Analytics
### SQL + Python + Power BI

## 📊 About
End-to-end sales analytics project analyzing 9994 sales records from a US retail superstore (2015-2018) to answer 30+ business questions 
using SQL, Python and Power BI.

## ❓ Business Questions Answered
- What is total revenue, profit and orders?
- Which region is most profitable?
- Which products are losing money?
- What is the impact of discount on profit?
- Which customer segment is most valuable?
- What are the monthly and yearly sales trends?
- Which states and cities are most profitable?

## 💡 Key Findings
- Store generated $2.3M revenue with only 12.03% profit margin
- West region is top performer at  21.95% profit margin
- Central region losing money at -10.41% margin despite $500K sales
- Orders with 0% discount generate $320,987 profit vs -$99,558 loss for 40%+ discount orders
- Cubify 3D Printers losing $12,719 combined due to 50%+ discounts
- November is consistently best month every year — clear seasonal pattern
- 5 out of top 10 states are losing moneyincluding Texas (-$25K loss)

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- SQL (SQLite)
- Power BI
- Jupyter Notebook (Kaggle)
